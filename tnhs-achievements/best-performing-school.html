Introduction: A New Era of Education

Talipan National High School is DepEd Quezon’s 2nd Best Performing School out of 96 competing secondary schools in this year’s Division Schools Press Conference (DSPC) held on September 12-14 in Lucban, Quezon.

The Talipeño Way

For months, Talipeño Press campus journalists poured their time, effort, and passion into campus journalism. They write not only to compete but also to live out their love for truth and storytelling.

All that dedication paid off as we proudly clinched Second Best Performing School in this year's Division Schools Press Conference (DSPC) held last September 12-14 in Lucban, Quezon. This is more than a win because it is a testament to the Talipeño way of writing with purpose, with heart, and with pride.

Now we carry the same fire as we march to Rizal for the first-ever Reconfigured Regional Schools Press Conference (RRSPC) JournCamp in February next year.

Conclusion: Talaga Namang Husay Sumasabay

In conclusion, Talipan National High School's achievement as the Second Best Performing School is not merely a competition result, but a powerful affirmation of the "Talipeño Way."
